Our Story

Why we built Traceline

Why has test management become so expensive?

Every software team deserves professional testing tools. Yet many teams — startups, students, nonprofits, independent developers, and small businesses — find themselves paying hundreds or even thousands of dollars every year just to manage test cases.

We experienced that firsthand. After years of working with commercial platforms, we kept asking the same question. We weren't looking for dozens of complicated enterprise modules — we wanted a tool that was fast, clean, modern, easy to learn, and powerful enough for real QA teams.

So we built Traceline. Not to compete with enterprise software — to give everyone access to professional-quality testing without locking them into expensive subscriptions.

Features

Everything a QA team actually uses

Test Case Management

CSV import with auto-classification, versioning, and shared steps.

Real-time Execution

Keyboard-driven runner — pass, fail, block, or retest without touching the mouse.

Requirements Traceability

Link requirements to test cases and see coverage rollups at a glance.

Issue Tracking

A real kanban board, plus one-click push to Jira when you find a bug.

AI-Assisted Testing

Generate test cases, summarize runs, and cluster failures — bring your own OpenAI-compatible endpoint, including local models.

Reports & Dashboards

Execution trends, coverage, and release health without a BI tool.

Integrations

Slack notifications, generic outbound webhooks, and a token-authenticated API for CI pipelines.

Built to be Self-Hosted

CSRF protection, rate limiting, encrypted secrets at rest — hardened, not an afterthought.

Full Source, No Lock-in

Plain PHP + MySQL. No framework tax, no black box, nothing you can't read.
